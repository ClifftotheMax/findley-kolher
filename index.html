Celebrating 75 Years

Get to Know Findley-Kolher Interiors

As of 2023, it is with humble pride, that our design firm celebrates 75 years in business. It is a true testament to good design and business to remain the longest-existing design firm in Ohio. We are always inspired to create highly personalized, stylish interiors that capture the warmth, joy, playfulness and function of our clients, in both homes and businesses.

From generation-to-generation Findley-Kohler Interiors has been adapting to the ever-changing needs and tastes of our community. We know the importance of “getting to know” a client, their family and their needs at home and we understand how it defines that a client's style. We view it as our responsibility as designers to implement the client's taste in a timeless fashion that maximizes the function. We meet every project with enthusiasm, curiosity and the experience to customize spaces for our valued customers, who have become more like family and friends than business relationships.

With our first location founded by Gordon Findley on Lane Ave. in Columbus, to our expanded location on East Broad St. near Bexley for 33 years, to our presence in Gahanna, first at 57 Granville St. and now our most recent relocation to 189-A Johnstown Rd., our design team has played an integral role in growing strong communities with each new endeavor.

As principals in Findley-Kohler Interiors, Doug and Rose Mary Kohler have worked to contribute and give back to our communities through their leadership and service. From events like the beautification of Olde Gahanna and the Creekside area, as well as the success of the Holiday Lights Parade in Gahanna on Thanksgiving weekend, to leadership roles in the Association of Interior Design (ASID) and Planned Parenthood, they have helped guide, foster and mentor the aspirations, dreams and lives of young creative people in design, the arts and education.

In 2019, Doug and Rose Mary Kohler were recognized by the Columbus Museum of Art with the first ever Lifetime Achievement Award for extraordinary contributions to interior design; created to recognize their generous and ongoing support to the community and industry.
